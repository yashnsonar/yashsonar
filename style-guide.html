Overall Formatting

The overall site colors are a light green background, dark green headings, blue links, and black text.

The general font-family for the site is Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif with backup fonts. The general text size is 16px.

Site Header / Branding

The header area uses a large dark green heading. The heading is centered and has spaced letters.

Navigation Links

Site navigation uses blue links without underlines removed. The navigation area has a light gray background, padding, and a black border.

Content Section

The webpage content section uses padding and line spacing to make the text easier to read.

Site Footer

The footer has a background color of light gray.
