Our Offices

There are two offices for your convenince. The main office is in downtown Seattle or we will meet with you at the Bellevue office by appointment.

Driving Directions:

  1. Seattle Office: 395 Legal Building, 2793 Positive Avenue
    1. From I-5 Southbound take the James St. exit. Turn right on Columbia. Go 2 1/2 blocks downhill and look on your right for the Legal Building Parking. Turn left into the garage.
    2. From I-5 Northbound take the James St. exit. Turn left on James. Go 2 blocks downhill. Turn right on 4th Avenue. Go 2 blocks. Turn left on Columbia. GO 1/2 block and look on your left for the Legal Building Parking. Turn left into the garage.
  2. Bellevue Office: 325 West Ave
    1. From 405 Northbound or Southbound take the exit at 6th Avenue. Go uphill towards courthouse. Just past courthouse, turn left on West Avenue. Go south 1 blocks and look for the address on your left.
